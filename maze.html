My Mazes made out of only HTML and CSS

Rules of the game

This is all in a Maze

Spikes will pop out of nowhere,there are 5 gems after you get all 5 gems you move on to the next level (Gems are purple squares).

This is what to expect

The spikes come out of nowhere and you have to double jump to get it

  • The 1st level has 1 spike to get to the final gem .
  • The 2nd level has 2 spikes guarding the final gem.
  • The 3rd level has 3 spikes guarding the final gem and those spikes come out of nowhere and you have to double jump to get it
  • The 4th level has 4 spikes guarding the final gem and those spikes come out of nowhere and you have to double jump to get it
  • The 5th level has 5 spikes guarding the final gem and those spikes come out of nowhere and you have to double jump to get it.
As you move there will be more and more spikes, spikes can come out of nowhere so be careful. You might have to jump into the yellow to get some gems.

Use the up arrow to move up, down arrow to move down, left arrow to move left, right arrow to move right, hold the up button to jump. Follow the white line to get to the end it can sometimes be misleading.
